# **Stage 1 — Business Understanding (CRISP-DM)**

## **Context**

Umbbra was hired to analyze the commercial performance of a fictitious company that sells 5 executive training programs.
This company provided 12 months of sales, marketing, and commercial performance data for diagnostic purposes.

The goal is to use Python and RevOps methods to:

* Identify bottlenecks in the funnel (Leads → MQL → SQL → Opportunities → Sales)
* Evaluate marketing efficiency (CPL, CAC, investment, pricing)
* Identify the best- and worst-performing products
* Analyze seasonality and behavioral patterns
* Structure recommendations for commercial optimization

---

## **Business Objective**

Generate insights that allow the company to:

1. Reduce CAC
2. Increase conversion rates across funnel stages
3. Identify products with the highest growth opportunity
4. Create a foundation for Umbbra’s upsell (performance marketing management)

---

## **Success Criteria**

The project will be considered successful if:

1. At least **three bottlenecks** in the commercial funnel are identified
2. We are able to propose **actionable strategic recommendations**
3. Clear data visualizations are produced (final report)
4. A strong pitch is derived for Umbbra’s presentation to the client

---

## **Deliverables for this Stage**

* Business map
* Objectives
* Success criteria
* Data file ready for analysis
